Expressions by Audrey

Expressions by Audrey is a vibrant collection of handcrafted wooden sign decor that brings personality and helps make any space your own. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and painted by hand, blending rustic charm with bold, expressive colors. Made to be durable and weather-resistant, these signs are perfect for adding a pop of inspiration indoors or making a statement in outdoor living areas. Whether you're decorating a cozy nook or brightening up your porch, Expressions offers artful decor that speaks from the heart.

Carmen Elise's Extraordinary Creations

Carmen Elise's Extraordinary Creations is a vibrant collection of handcrafted bracelets made with care and creativity. Featuring an eclectic mix of plastic beads, hand-crafted polymer designs, and durable paracord, each piece is designed to stand out and express individuality. With a wide variety of styles and colors—and new seasonal collections on the way—there’s something unique for everyone in this ever-evolving collection of wearable art.

She is currently building her inventory and we look forward to showcasing her lineup within the coming weeks!
